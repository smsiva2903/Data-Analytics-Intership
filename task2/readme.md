---

##  Key Insights / Story  
- **Technology** category drives maximum sales.  
- **Furniture** has low profit and contains loss-making items like *Tables*.  
- **West Region** contributes the highest revenue.  
- Sales increase during **Q4**, showing seasonal behavior.  
- A small set of customers contribute a large portion of total sales.  
- Standard shipping is the most preferred mode.
